Backer Agreement

Last Updated: October 2, 2025

1. Introduction

This Backer Agreement ("Agreement") governs the relationship between LaunchPad Market ("Platform", "we", "us", or "our") and backers ("Backer", "you", or "your") who support projects on our platform.

2. Acceptance of Terms

By backing a project on LaunchPad Market, you agree to be bound by this Agreement and all applicable laws and regulations. If you do not agree to these terms, you may not use our platform to back projects.

3. Understanding Crowdfunding

As a backer, you understand and acknowledge that:

  • Crowdfunding involves financial risk and projects may not be successfully funded
  • Even successfully funded projects may face delays or challenges in development
  • You may not receive the product as described or within the expected timeframe
  • Your payment is authorized but not charged until a project reaches its funding goal
  • If a project fails to reach its funding goal, no charges will be made

4. Backer Responsibilities

Project Selection

As a backer, you are responsible for:

  • Reviewing project descriptions and creator information carefully
  • Understanding the risks associated with each project
  • Ensuring your payment information is accurate and up-to-date
  • Providing accurate shipping information for physical products
  • Communicating with creators respectfully and professionally

Payment Terms

When backing a project, you agree to:

  • Authorize payment if the project reaches its funding goal
  • Reserve products by placing orders
  • Understand that orders are processed through our platform

5. Rights and Benefits

Project Updates

As a backer, you have the right to:

  • Receive regular updates from creators on project progress
  • Ask questions and receive responses from creators
  • Be notified of any significant delays or changes to the project
  • Receive your rewards as described in the project listing

6. Refund Policy

Pre-Funding Deadline

Before a project reaches its funding deadline:

  • You may cancel your backing at any time
  • Full refunds will be issued for cancelled backings
  • No fees or penalties apply for cancellations

Post-Funding Success

After a project successfully reaches its funding goal:

  • Your order is confirmed but not yet processed
  • You may cancel within 48 hours of funding completion
  • After 48 hours, standard cancellation policies apply

7. Platform Protections

LaunchPad Market provides the following protections for backers:

  • All orders are held securely until project funding success
  • No orders are processed for projects that fail to reach their funding goal
  • Dispute resolution services for issues with creators or products
  • Verification processes for creator identities and project legitimacy
  • Clear communication channels between backers and creators

8. Backer Obligations

Communication

As a backer, you agree to:

  • Communicate with creators respectfully and professionally
  • Provide accurate and up-to-date contact information
  • Respond to creator communications in a timely manner
  • Report any issues or concerns through proper channels

9. Disclaimers and Limitations

LaunchPad Market makes no warranties regarding:

  • The success of any project or likelihood of funding
  • The quality, safety, or legality of creator products
  • The accuracy of any project descriptions or claims
  • The ability of creators to deliver on their promises
  • The timeline for product development or delivery

10. Limitation of Liability

In no event shall LaunchPad Market be liable for:

  • Failure of creators to deliver products as promised
  • Delays in product development or delivery
  • Defective or unsatisfactory products
  • Financial losses incurred through project backing
  • Indirect, incidental, or consequential damages

11. Dispute Resolution

If you have a dispute with a creator:

  • First attempt to resolve the issue directly with the creator
  • Contact our support team at support@launchpadmarket.com if direct resolution fails
  • Provide detailed information about the dispute and any relevant documentation
  • Our team will investigate and mediate the dispute
  • We may request additional information from both parties

12. Account Termination

LaunchPad Market reserves the right to:

  • Suspend or terminate backer accounts for policy violations
  • Restrict access to certain features or services
  • Cancel or reverse transactions in cases of fraud or abuse
